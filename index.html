Intel // FAQ

Q: How do I apply these to my desktop?

Windows doesn't support live wallpapers natively. You need to use a software engine. We highly recommend Wallpaper Engine (Steam, $4) or Lively Wallpaper (Open Source, Free). Simply drag and drop the downloaded .mp4 file into the app.

Q: How do I apply these to my phone?

Most modern Android and iOS devices allow you to set an .mp4 as your lock screen directly from your gallery app. For home screens, you may need a free third-party app like "Video Live Wallpaper".
