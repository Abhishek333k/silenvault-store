Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

At SilenVault, we stand behind the quality of our digital assets. However, due to the inherent nature of downloadable digital goods, we enforce a strict refund policy to protect our creators and our platform.

1. All Sales are Final

Because digital products cannot be "returned" once they are downloaded, all sales are considered final and non-refundable. By completing your purchase and downloading the asset files, you waive your right to a refund.

2. Exceptions to the Rule

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. Refunds will ONLY be considered under the following highly specific circumstances:

  • Corrupted or Defective Files: The asset file is demonstrably broken, corrupted, or completely unusable, and our support team is unable to provide you with a working replacement within 48 hours of your report.
  • Non-Delivery: You did not receive the download link due to a system error, and we are unable to manually deliver the files to your email.
  • Duplicate Purchases: You accidentally purchased the exact same digital asset twice within a 24-hour period.

3. Merchant of Record Processing

Our order process is conducted by our online reseller, Lemon Squeezy. Lemon Squeezy is the Merchant of Record for all our orders. If a refund is approved under our exceptions, Lemon Squeezy will process the return of funds to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, this can take 3-5 business days.

4. How to Request Help

If you believe your situation qualifies for an exception, please do not file a chargeback with your bank, as this will result in a permanent ban from SilenVault. Instead, contact us immediately at: support@silenvault.com with your Order ID and a detailed explanation of the issue.
